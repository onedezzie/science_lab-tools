Tripod-Stand

A Tripod Stand

DESCRIPTION:

A laboratory tripod is a portable, three-legged platform equipment, which is usually made of lightweight metal such as stainless steel and Aluminium so that it can be moved conveniently within the lab.

USES:

1. The main usage is to support or hold the flasks and beakers during experiments.

2. They are necessary to hold things to be heated above a Bunsen burner.
