Pipette

A Pipette

DESCRIPTION:

A pipette, or chemical dropper, is a small glass or plastic tube used to transfer measurable amounts of liquids from one container to another. French scientist Louis Pasteur developed a range of pipettes.

HOW TO USE:

Using a volumeric pipette involves dipping the pipette into a solution and placing a finger over the end, or squeezing a rubber bulb to draw up the specified amount.

USES:

For dropping in titration
